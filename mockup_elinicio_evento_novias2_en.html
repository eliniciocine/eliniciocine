September 2025 Southern Brides

Retrospective + talk

Centro Cultural de España · Mexico City

The Sala Panorama at Centro Cultural de España en México hosted a retrospective dedicated to Elena's documentary work, featuring screenings of her short films Pueblo (2015), Las vísceras (2016), and Those Who Desire (2018), along with a screening of Southern Brides.

This program was accompanied by a conversation between Elena López Riera, the audience, and Mexican writer Natalia Durand.
