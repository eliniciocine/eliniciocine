About Us

We are el inicio cine, a Mexican film distribution company that supports films in reaching exhibition spaces across Mexico. We design and plan programming circuits, develop communication strategies, build partnerships, and create educational activities to celebrate the life of films. We are driven by the belief that other forms of distribution are possible — distribution that is not voracious but caring; distribution that places the film and its possibilities beyond the screen at the center.

Mare Sánchez-Armáss

Distribution

She has coordinated and planned the distribution of more than 30 national and international fiction, documentary, and animated films within both commercial and independent circuits in Mexico, working with distribution companies such as Interior XIII and Pimienta Films. Trained at the Industry Academy of the Locarno Film Festival, the Públicos del Futuro Seminar at FICUNAM, and the Intensive Distribution Clinic of the Ibermedia Program. She has also collaborated as a consultant with La Corriente del Golfo, Ambulante, Machete Cine, and others.

Arantxa Luna

Communications & Partnerships

Writer, researcher, and cultural manager specializing in film. She has collaborated with FICUNAM, Ambulante Documentary Tour, FICM, and the Mar del Plata International Film Festival. Author of essayistic texts in Nexos, Letras Libres, Revista de la Universidad, and Cine PREMIÈRE. She has developed communication strategies, press management, and educational partnerships for titles by Jim Jarmusch, Claire Denis, Carla Simón, Nicolás Pereda, and others.

Services
01

Distribution

We support films from their release through their life in Mexico's exhibition circuits. We plan programming circuits, manage exhibition spaces, and build partnerships to connect each film with its audience.

02

Digital strategy

We design the digital presence of films: communication, social media, launch campaigns, and materials. We create strategies that accompany the film's life before, during, and after its release.

03

Consulting

We offer specialized advisory services in film distribution and communication for production companies, festivals, and filmmakers seeking to map their exhibition path with intention and clarity.
