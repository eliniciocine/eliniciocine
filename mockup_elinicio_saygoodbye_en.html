Say Goodbye
Paloma López Carrillo · 2025 · Mexico · Documentary
Visions Du Réel 2025  ·  FICUNAM · Ahora México 2025  ·  Youth Jury Award · Ecuador

Beneath snow-covered mountains, a family confronts the void left by disappearance. Say Goodbye follows Rosa, Sol, and Javier on an intimate journey where silence, memory, and love intertwine to shape grief. A story about an absence that never fully leaves.
