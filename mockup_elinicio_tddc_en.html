Every Document of Civilization
Tatiana Mazú González · 2024 · Argentina · Documentary, Experimental
FICUNAM – Silver Puma for Best Film and Audience Award  ·  FID Marseille – Georges de Beauregard Award  ·  FICValdivia – Special Jury Mention  ·  FICViña – Best Director Award

The story of a mother who lost her son at the hands of the police confronts the image of normality. Her struggle and her voice evoke the imagined worlds of Jules Verne, with which they once dreamed together. This film is a process of excavation — or the dissection of a landscape where, sixteen years ago, the Argentine state made Luciano Arruga disappear.

November 2025
Special screening + conversation
Centro de Cultura Digital · Mexico City

In partnership with Centro de Cultura Digital and the collective Antes Muertx Cine, a special screening of Every Document of Civilization, directed by Tatiana Mazú, was held. Following the screening, Tatiana Mazú was joined in conversation by Mexican journalist Daniela Rea and activist and scholar Alicia de los Ríos.
