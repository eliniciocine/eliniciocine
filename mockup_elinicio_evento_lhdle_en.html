February 2026 The Hour of the Star

Special screening + talk

Cine Lido, Centro Cultural Bella Época · Mexico City

In partnership with the Guimarães Rosa Institute Mexico of the Government of Brazil and Fondo de Cultura Económica, a special screening of The Hour of the Star, directed by Suzana Amaral, was held at Cine Lido, Centro Cultural Bella Época, in Mexico City.

Following the screening, Mexican director María Novaro, alongside writer Brenda Lozano and Brazilian cinema researcher Lilia Lustosa, engaged in a conversation about the connections between literature and film, focusing in particular on the intersections between the work of Suzana Amaral and Clarice Lispector.
